Welcome Crypto Movers!

Cryptocurrency is fascinating because of how greatly the value can vacillate during the course of the day. In a day’s time, a cryptocurrency can experience a 30% increase or 50% decrease, for example.

This site ranks 50 cryptocurrencies based on the percent the value changed that day.

You can click on a currency or type the name of a cryptocurrency in the search bar to obtain the symbol, current price, percentage change, market cap,volume, and open price in the past 24 hours. You can also click on the articles on the right for the latest in crypto news.

All data was provided by the Cryptocompare API. Designed and coded by Sean Harte.
